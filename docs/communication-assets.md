## Press Release Template

### FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**Arakal Industries Launches New Website to Enhance Customer Experience**

[City, Date] - Arakal Industries, a leading towel manufacturer based in Solapur, Maharashtra, today announced the launch of its newly redesigned website. The new website reflects the company's commitment to innovation, quality, and customer service.

**About the New Website:**
The new website features a modern, professional design that showcases Arakal Industries' complete product range, manufacturing capabilities, and sustainability initiatives. Key features include:
- Comprehensive product catalog
- Enhanced navigation and user experience
- Improved mobile responsiveness
- Streamlined contact and quote request process
- Resource library with downloadable materials

**Company Background:**
Arakal Industries has a rich heritage in the textile industry, spanning over 80 years. The company started with handlooms in 1940, expanded into jacquard chaddars in 1965, launched terry towel production in 1984, and established Arakal Industries in 1997.

**Production Capabilities:**
With an annual production capacity of 800-1000 tons, Arakal Industries operates two modern manufacturing facilities in Solapur, Maharashtra. The company produces premium quality Terry Towels, Dobby Towels, and Jacquard Chaddars for customers worldwide.

**Sustainability:**
Arakal Industries is committed to sustainable manufacturing practices and environmental responsibility, with initiatives focused on water conservation, energy efficiency, and waste reduction.

### Interview Guide (Question Themes)
1. **Vision & Strategy**
   - What does success look like 6–12 months post-launch?
   - Which markets or product lines are priority for growth?
2. **Customers & Sales**
   - Who are the top buyer personas? What problems are we solving for them?
   - How do leads currently arrive (directories, referrals, trade shows)? Where are the friction points?
3. **Products & Operations**
   - What towel categories, customizations, or certifications differentiate Arakal?
   - What capacity, lead times, and quality assurances should we highlight?
4. **Brand & Content**
   - How should the brand feel (tone, imagery, heritage vs. innovation balance)?
   - Which stories, testimonials, or case studies resonate most with prospects?
5. **Technology & Analytics**
   - Which tools are in use today (CRM, email, analytics)? What needs improvement?
   - What insights do stakeholders expect from the website post-launch?
6. **Risks & Constraints**
   - Are there compliance, privacy, or operational constraints to account for?
   - What worries stakeholders about the current digital presence?
